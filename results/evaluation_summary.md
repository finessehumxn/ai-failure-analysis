# Evaluation Summary

## Model
Baseline Rule-Based Classifier

## Dataset Size
5 labeled test samples

## Accuracy
40%

## Key Observations
- High failure rate on ambiguous emotional language  
- Inability to detect implicit distress signals  
- Over-reliance on explicit keywords  

## Risk Implications
In mental health contexts, these failures could lead to:
- Dismissing individuals in distress  
- Misinterpreting emotional states  
- Providing inappropriate or unsafe responses  

## Conclusion
The baseline model demonstrates limited reliability in human-centered scenarios, reinforcing the need for more context-aware and robust AI systems.
